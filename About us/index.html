Who are we?

The environment surrounding food, such as consumers' tastes and lifestyles, globalization and expansion of the Internet, is changing consistently and rapidly.
It is our mandate to bring together the small pieces that makes it a chain, from the prodution to the consumption and in so doing we link hotels, from small to large to the customer so as to improve growth and satisfy the customers by meeting their needs.
We will continuously respect our corporate commitment "Trust", and endeavor to achieve new innovations as a valuable corporate group that serves customers, in the pursuit of our corporate philosophy: "Abiding spirit and innovating power - We deliver enriched lifestyles through the medium of food".

Where are we?

We started of as a small company in Nairobi Kenya in May 2004 as a food supply group, moving from one office to another distributing lunch orders. It wasn't easy while starting but with time we have had so much experiences in what we do as a company.We have grown over the years reaching out to countries like South Africa, Ethiopia and Uganda.
We expect to grow by 30% within the next 3 years driven by our core values and principles.

What services do we offer?

As a food company we offer many services which include:
We deliver both cooked and raw food to our customers.
We connect our customers to restaurants around them both local and international with all that the restaurants offer, from their menu to their services to the prices.
Lastly our online website platform gives access to those who are willing to learn how to cook dishes of their choices by providing a recipes and tuitorial from the best of our chefs.
